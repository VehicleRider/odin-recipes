Dive into a Bowl of Teryaki and Rice

Key Ingredients

Chicken and Mirinade

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey (or brown sugar)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Bowl Components

  • 2 cups cooked white or brown rice
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Step by Step

  1. Mix soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil. Coat chicken and marinate for 20+ minutes (or overnight).
  2. Heat a pan over medium-high. Cook chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until caramelized. Slice into strips.
  3. Fill bowls with rice, top with chicken, broccoli, carrots, and cucumber. Drizzle with extra sauce, sprinkle sesame seeds and green onions.
