A Big Plate of Pasta

Rich, velvety pasta coated in a garlicky Parmesan sauce that’s quick enough for a weeknight but fancy enough to feel special. Comfort food at its finest.

Key Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine or penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or half-and-half)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (+ extra for garnish)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Step by Step

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente (per package instructions). Drain, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds (try not to burn). Pour in heavy cream, stir, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to low, stir in Parmesan, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning until smooth.
  4. Toss cooked pasta into the sauce, adding reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  5. Top with extra Parmesan and parsley.
