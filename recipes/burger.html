Building a Good Burger

Key Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Chili Flakes
  • One egg yolk

Additional Ingrediants

  • 4 burger buns (brioche or sesame)
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese (or American cheese)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)

Step by Step

  1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into patties (slightly larger than the buns, as they shrink).
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side (for medium). Add cheese in the last minute to melt.
  3. Butter the buns and toast them lightly in a pan or on the grill.
  4. Spread condiments on the buns, add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and the cheesy patty. Serve hot.
